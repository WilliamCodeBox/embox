# Integral Relations for Vectors

## Overview

* Line integral of vector field

$$
\int_a^b\vec{F}\cdot d\vec{l}
$$

Line integral of vector field $\vec{F}$ along a prescribed path from one location $a$ to another location $b$.

* Surface integral of vector field

$$
\int_{\Delta S}\vec{F}\cdot d\vec{s}
$$

Surface integral of vector field $\vec{F}$ through a surface $\Delta S$

* Volume integral of a density function

$$
\int_{\Delta V}\rho_v dv
$$

Volume integral of a density $\rho_v$ over the volume $\Delta V$
